---
title: Beige Stew
subtitle: _Dish for 4 people_
---

**Ingridients:**

- 2 chicken breast (450g)
- 1 bell pepper (120g)
- 3 large carrots (300g)
- Bacon, 200g
- 400g ris
- 100g of onions
- 165g of pickled bell peppers
- 500g of sour cream

> **Tip:**
> Use frozen onions, it saves time and doesn't matter much when they're fried.

> **Note:**
> Fresh bellpepper is better, but more cumbersome. Squash is also good.

**Steps:**

1. Start cooking the rice.
2. Fry the bacon and onions then take them off the heat into a bowl.
3. Fry the entire chicken breast on high heat, in the bacon fat, for a few minutes until nice and browned.
4. Take the chicken off the heat and cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Put them in the bowl.
5. Put everything in the bowl and all other unused ingredients in the bowl and let it stew for 10 minutes.
